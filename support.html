Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access Pro calculators?

You can purchase individual calculators for $0.99 each, or subscribe to RT Pro for full access: $4.99/month, $29.99/year, or $59.99 lifetime.

Can I use RT Pro offline?

Yes! All calculators and reference modules work offline once downloaded.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Your subscription or lifetime purchase will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → RT Pro, then cancel.
